CPR Guidelines

Infant (0-12 months) Chest compressions with two fingers, to about 1/3 of the depth of the chest. Cover patients nose and mouth with your mouth during mouth to mouth, and use small breaths. 30 compressions for every two breaths.

Child (1-8 years) Compress 1/3 of the way into the chest, using only the heel of one hand. 30 compressions for every two breaths.

Adult (>8 years) Compress 10cm into the chest, using two hands. 30 compressions for every two breaths.
