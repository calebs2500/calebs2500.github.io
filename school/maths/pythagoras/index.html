Pythagoras Theorem

Spreadsheet

What does it do?
This spreadsheet that I made can return missing values of length for sides of a right angled triangle, using Pythagoras. It also has the ability to create triads.

How can I use it?
For calculating missing values, enter the given two into the applicable boxes, and the third should display your missing length.

For calculating triads, enter two numbers into the m and n cells, but make sure that m is always larger than n. The sheet will give feedback about the validity of the triad. It will generate a triad, and the value of it's three sides.
